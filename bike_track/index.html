This is listed on the Selby Planning Portal as application number 2020/0225/FULM | Proposed change of use from grazing agricultural land to BMX cycle track with toilet block, picnic area and car park | Land South Of Gloster Close Busk Lane Church Fenton Tadcaster North Yorkshire

There is a search button on the link above - if you put the application reference into that Search bar, and press Search - it will bring up the application. Please support this application. It is a great opportunity for the village to have a safe place where children can learn to ride a bike, away from the busy roads, and should have no impact on nearby residents.

Alternatively, you can simply email planningcomments@selby.gov.uk and express your support for this application
